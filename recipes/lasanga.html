Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a tasty meal that's easy to make.
You find all you need to know in the step by step guid below

Ingredients

  • 2 Onions
  • 2 Cloves of garlic
  • 500 g Ground beef
  • 1 Tbsp Oil
  • 4 Tbsp Tomato paste
  • 500 g Crushed tomatoes
  • 8 Lasagna plates

Sauce

  • 6 Tbsp Butter
  • 6 Tbsp Flour
  • 10 Dl Milk
  • 2 Dl parmesan

Steps

  1. Turn on the oven on 175Â°C
  2. Dice the onions. cook the onions in the Oil in a frying.
    Add Tomato paste and the ground meat fry for a couple minutes and add
    the crushed tomato's let it cook for 10 minutes
  3. Sauce: Melt butter and stir in the flour. Dilute with the milk while whisking.
    Let the sauce boil for about 5 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Alternate the sauce, lasagne plates and the minced meat sauce in an oven-safe
    form (approx. 20 x 30 cm). Finish with sauce and parmesan cheese.
  5. Put the lasagna in the middle of the oven for about 40 minutes.
  6. To serve: Serve the lasagna with salad.
